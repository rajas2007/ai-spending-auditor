# Pricing Assumptions Used in the Audit Engine

The audit engine intentionally uses simplified pricing assumptions for the MVP.

Examples include:
- monthly per-seat assumptions
- generalized API usage estimates
- simplified team pricing calculations

The goal was to provide:
- directional optimization insights
- overlap detection
- spend visibility

rather than procurement-grade financial accounting.

- Enterprise pricing was treated as custom pricing where exact public pricing was unavailable.
- API-direct usage calculations were generalized because real-world token consumption varies heavily across organizations.
- Pricing values may change after the submission week because AI tooling pricing changes frequently.
