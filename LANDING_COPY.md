# FAQ

## 1. Does this require creating an account?

No. Audits can be generated anonymously. Email capture happens only after value is shown.

---

## 2. Is the pricing data accurate?

The audit engine uses pricing data verified from official vendor pricing pages during the submission week. Some enterprise/API pricing is estimated using simplified assumptions.

---

## 3. Does Aethra replace financial accounting tools?

No. Aethra is designed as a lightweight optimization and visibility tool, not a procurement or accounting platform.

---

## 4. Why would teams share these reports?

The reports are designed to simplify discussions around AI tooling overlap, operational efficiency, and subscription optimization.

---

## 5. How often would a company use this?

Most teams would likely use the tool periodically when reevaluating tooling decisions, budgeting, or onboarding new AI products.
